• Layover with a Local

    Turn your transfer into a trip

Client

KLM Royal Dutch Airlines

Project

Mobile Application

KLM Layover with a Local turns transfers into trips. It's connecting the travelers with a stop in Amsterdam airport the opportunity to meet up with a local and get a taste of Amsterdam.

70% of travelers have a layover in Amsterdam

With Amsterdam Central Station just 15 minutes away, why not make use of it? Adventure is just around the corner.

By connecting travelers to locals we turn waiting time into wander time, and for KLM it’s just another way to take you somewhere.

A match made in heaven

At the heart of the app is a smart matchmaking mechanism that matches travelers to locals.

Using their social profile, interests, shared languages and availability we made sure they would hit it off right away.

A personal layover guide for travelers

Leaving the airport during a layover is potentially stressful. That’s why we created a personal layover guide that gives the traveler step-by-step directions.

The layover guide is made up from dynamic cards that offer relevant actions at the appropiate times. Real-time notifications ensure that the traveler returns to the airport in time.

An all-inclusive mini trip

In collaboration with the national railway company we offer the traveler a free train ticket into the city.

But there is more. At selected bars, they can get their first drink for free, available through an in-app coupon.

So, how do we get the locals to sign up?

We’re targeting locals that have an intrinsic motivation to meet up. Language students, for example, can practice their italian with a real Italian.

In collaboration with language schools, the expat centre and local bars we make sure to find locals that are excited for a meetup. One lucky local will win a ticket to the country of the traveler.

Campaign objective

Get people to visit the campaign site, without nearly any paid media.

Activation objective

Get locals and travelers to sign up for the pilot.

Brand objective

Strengthen KLM’s service and innovation perception for passengers with a layover.

Campaign results

200.000 unique visitors.

Activation results

1500 signups in pilot.

Based on a 3 month pilot, limited to passengers from Italy, USA and Canada with a layover of more than 6 hours. Potential pitfall was a lack of locals willing to act as a guide, but we reached a healthy 2-to-1 ratio of locals-to-travelers.

Brand results

Over 50 articles in international business, innovation and travel publications.

The KLM Layover with a Local app perfectly matches the KLM strategy by offering excellent and personalized service.
